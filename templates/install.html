How to Install

iPhone & iPad

Open with the Safari browser.

  1. 1 Tap the Share button (a square with an arrow pointing up).
  2. 2 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. 3 Confirm by tapping "Add" in the top corner.

Android

Open with the Chrome browser.

  1. 1 Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  2. 2 Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. 3 Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the installation.

Desktop Computer

Open with Chrome or Edge.

  1. 1 Look for the Install icon (a screen with a down-arrow) in the address bar.
  2. 2 Click the icon and then click "Install".
  3. 3 The app will now appear in your computer's app list.
